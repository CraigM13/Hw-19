THE REVIVAL STORY

Sonia Rife

Owner & Creative Director

I started Revival Vintage like many small businesses by doing pop-up markets, launching an Etsy online store, and running a small booth at the local Antique Mall. However, in March 2013 I took the leap and opened a small brick and mortar store - yay! Revival Vintage was even chosen as the "Best New Kid on the Vintage Block" by readers of The Austin Chronicle our first year in the shop.
